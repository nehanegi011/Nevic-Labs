Personalized, agentic AI

An agent that markets one patient at a time.

Most healthcare marketing broadcasts the same message to everyone. Nevic Reach runs an agentic AI layer that reasons about each patient's context and decides the next best message, channel and moment — on its own.

Context-aware targeting

The agent reads specialty, past visits and local search intent to decide who to reach and with what offer.

Autonomous campaign loops

Content, targeting and spend adjust on their own from what's converting — no waiting on a monthly report.

1:1 personalized outreach

Each patient's message is generated for their language, specialty interest and stage — not a template blast.

Hand-off into the thread

Once a patient shows real intent, the agent moves them straight into a Nevic Connect booking conversation.
