Privacy policy


1. Who we are

Nevic Labs Private Limited is a healthcare software company based in Pune, India. We build patient engagement and healthcare provider operations software for healthcare providers of every size.

2. What we collect

  • Healthcare Provider data — details of your practice, doctors, schedules and patients you enter into our software.
  • Patient data — names, contact numbers, appointment history and, where enabled, clinical notes.
  • Website data — name, email, phone and message when you contact us or request a demo.

3. How we use it

We use healthcare provider and patient data solely to run the services you've asked us to run — bookings, reminders, follow-ups, records and analytics for your healthcare provider. We do not sell or share your data with third parties, and we do not use it for advertising.

4. Where it lives

All data is stored on secure servers hosted in India, encrypted at rest and in transit. Access is strictly limited to authorized Nevic Labs staff on a need-to-know basis, with full audit logging.

5. Consent-first

We only process patient data for the purposes you've asked us to — no analytics profiling, no third-party sharing, no marketing use. Consent, when applicable, is captured explicitly and can be withdrawn at any time.

6. Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time by writing to privacy@neviclabs.in. We respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies for basic functioning. We do not run tracking, advertising or third-party analytics scripts.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Write to privacy@neviclabs.in or reach us at the address in the contact section above.
