About Us

Elevating the everyday experience of healthcare.

Nevic Labs was founded by two technology leaders with a combined 38+ years of experience building and scaling softwares, now focused on solving the operational challenges faced by healthcare providers across India.

Our mission

Software that disappears into a calmer healthcare provider day.

We believe the biggest lever in Indian healthcare isn't a new app patients have to learn — it's fixing the operational chaos behind the front desk and meeting patients on the channel they already trust. Everything we build works toward that one outcome.

Our story

Why we started Nevic Labs.

Our Story


After decades of building and scaling enterprise software for organizations around the world, our founders saw a powerful opportunity much closer to home. Across India's healthcare system, exceptional clinical care was often held back by fragmented operations, manual processes, and disconnected patient experiences.

We started Nevic Labs with a simple belief: healthcare teams should spend more time caring for patients—not managing paperwork, chasing follow-ups, or coordinating across disconnected systems.

Our journey begins with the everyday realities of the OPD and the patient experience, building intelligent technology that removes operational friction, strengthens patient relationships, and empowers healthcare providers to deliver better care at every step.
