FAQ

Answers to the questions we hear most.

Do my patients need to install anything?
No. Nevic Connect works entirely inside WhatsApp, which your patients already use every day. There is no app to download and no new account to create.
What languages do you support?
Marathi, Hindi and English out of the box. We can add other Indian languages on request during onboarding.
How long does onboarding take?
Most healthcare providers are live within 5–7 working days. This includes WhatsApp Business API setup, doctor calendars, message templates and front-desk training.
Where is my healthcare provider's data stored?
All data is stored on secure servers hosted in India, encrypted at rest and in transit. Access is strictly limited to authorized Nevic Labs staff on a need-to-know basis, with full audit logging.
Is my patient data safe?
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption, role-based access, and never sell or share patient data. Every access to sensitive data is logged.
What does it cost?
Nevic Labs is priced per healthcare provider, per month, based on the doctors and message volume you need. Transparent, tiered plans — no per-seat surprises. Detailed pricing sheets are being finalised; get in touch and we'll send you the current tier that fits your practice.
Is there a free trial or pilot?
Yes. We run a structured 30-day pilot for most new healthcare providers — full product, real patients, real numbers. If the ROI isn't visible by day 30, you walk away with no charge.
Do you integrate with our existing EHR or HIS?
Yes. We offer API-based integrations that connect Nevic Labs with existing healthcare provider-management, HIS, LIS, and EMR systems. Bookings, patient records and reminders sync automatically so nothing gets entered twice.
Can we bulk-shift or cancel appointments?
Yes. When a doctor is unavailable, one action reschedules or cancels every affected appointment — and every patient gets an automatic WhatsApp notification with the new slot or refund path.
Does it work for multi-doctor and multi-department setups?
Yes. Nevic Pulse handles unlimited doctors and departments in one dashboard, with a live queue, a shared calendar and per-doctor reporting.
Can patients respond, reschedule and upload documents on WhatsApp?
Yes. Every message is two-way. Patients can confirm, reschedule, cancel, fill digital forms, and even upload prescriptions or reports — all inside the same chat.
Do you support telemedicine?
Yes. Nevic Connect can send consultation links, pre-consult forms and post-consult follow-ups over WhatsApp — closing the loop on virtual visits the same way we do for in-person.
Can labs share reports securely through Nevic?
Yes. Once results are ready, reports go to the patient on WhatsApp as a secure attachment — no lab trip just to collect a printout, no front-desk load.
Are you compliant with India's data protection laws?
Yes. Architecture is aligned with India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act — data hosted in India, encrypted at rest and in transit, consent captured explicitly, access strictly audited. We also follow international healthcare privacy standards for healthcare providers with cross-border operations.
Does WhatsApp itself keep patient messages private?
Yes. Every message runs on Meta's official WhatsApp Business API with end-to-end encryption — the same secure infrastructure banks and airlines use. Meta doesn't read the content, and Nevic Labs only stores what your healthcare provider needs to run its operations.
